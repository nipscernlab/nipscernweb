# THE ARCHITECT — Year X
### Future Shelf & Permanent References

Books and references that do not belong to the six-year arc (2026–2031). Each item has a clear **life trigger** or a **consultative purpose** — they enter the active plan when the moment arrives, or stay available on demand.

---

## Life-Cycle Reading by Trigger

### When pregnancy begins

**The Expectant Father** — Armin Brott
The paternal companion to *Expecting Better* (Year II). Month by month of gestation through the father's lens — what is happening with the mother, with the fetus, and what the father can and should do.

### With a newborn (0–5)

**Cribsheet** — Emily Oster
The evidence-based continuation of *Expecting Better* for the first five years. Breastfeeding vs. formula, sleep, vaccines, daycare, screens — early childhood decisions with the rigor of data.

### With a child at home (0–12)

**The Whole-Brain Child** — Daniel Siegel & Tina Payne Bryson
Cognitive and emotional development 0–12 through neuroscience. How a child's brain matures and how to accompany it without becoming a helicopter parent.

### With a daughter entering adolescence

**Untangled: Guiding Teenage Girls Through the Seven Transitions into Adulthood** — Lisa Damour
Clinical psychologist of adolescent girls. The seven typical transitions — parting from childhood, new social worlds, emerging sexuality, autonomy, identity — mapped with nuance and without panic.

## Permanent Reference

**MSD Manual — Consumer Version**
[msdmanuals.com/pt-br/casa](https://msdmanuals.com/pt-br/casa)

The successor of the classic *Merck Manual for Consumers*, in Portuguese, free. Covers diseases, symptoms, prevention, treatment, and medications in rigorous lay language. **Used by consultation on demand** — there is no need to read it cover to cover. Bookmark on phone and browser.

## Why this file exists

These items live outside the six years because their value depends on a **biographical context** (an actual baby to raise, a real pregnancy to navigate) or on **eventual use** (a medical question in lay terms). Read in abstraction, outside the relevant phase, they become trivia, or weight without return. Registered here so they are not forgotten when the trigger arrives.
