Fields of Logic Reference

A comprehensive collection of logic fields and their foundational texts

This reference provides a comprehensive overview of logic fields throughout history, from Aristotle to present day, along with their foundational texts or articles for further reading.

Classical Logic

Aristotelian Logic (Syllogistic Logic)

Book: "Prior Analytics" by Aristotle (4th century BCE)

Stoic Logic (Propositional Logic)

Book: "The Logical Part of Chrysippus' Teaching" (fragments collected in "Stoicorum Veterum Fragmenta" by Hans von Arnim)

Megarian Logic

Book: "Studies in Logic and Probability" by George Boole discusses Megarian contributions

Buddhist Logic (Hetuvidya)

Book: "Pramāṇavārttika" by Dharmakīrti (7th century)

Traditional Chinese Logic (School of Names, Mohist Logic)

Book: "The Mohist Canon" (Mozi)

Medieval Developments

Scholastic Logic

Book: "Summulae Logicales" by Peter of Spain (13th century)

Medieval Modal Logic

Book: "De Modalibus" by William of Ockham

Supposition Theory

Book: "Tractatus de Suppositionibus" by William of Ockham

Consequences Theory

Book: "De Consequentiis" by John Buridan (14th century)

Obligationes (Disputational Logic)

Book: "Tractatus de Obligationibus" by Walter Burley (early 14th century)

Modern Classical Logic

Propositional Logic (Truth-Functional Logic)

Book: "Begriffsschrift" by Gottlob Frege (1879)

First-Order Logic (Predicate Logic)

Book: "Principia Mathematica" by Whitehead & Russell (1910-1913)

Second-Order Logic

Book: "Second-order Logic and Foundations of Mathematics" by Väänänen (2001)

Higher-Order Logic

Book: "Simple Type Theory" by Alonzo Church (1940)

Set Theory

Book: "Grundlagen der Mengenlehre" by Ernst Zermelo (1908)

Non-Classical Logics

Modal Logic (Alethic, Deontic, Epistemic, Temporal)

Book: "Symbolic Logic" by C.I. Lewis & C.H. Langford (1932)

Many-Valued Logic

Book: "On three-valued logic" by Jan Łukasiewicz (1920)

Fuzzy Logic

Book: "Fuzzy Sets" by Lotfi Zadeh (1965)

Intuitionistic Logic

Book: "Zur Intuitionistischen Arithmetik und Zahlentheorie" by Arend Heyting (1930)

Relevance Logic

Book: "Entailment: The Logic of Relevance and Necessity" by Anderson & Belnap (1975)

Paraconsistent Logic

Book: "Paraconsistent Logic: Essays on the Inconsistent" by Priest, Routley & Norman (1989)

Quantum Logic

Book: "The Logic of Quantum Mechanics" by Birkhoff & von Neumann (1936)

Free Logic

Book: "Free Logic: Selected Essays" by Karel Lambert (2002)

Non-Monotonic Logic

Book: "Non-monotonic Reasoning" by McDermott & Doyle (1980)

Default Logic

Book: "A Logic for Default Reasoning" by Raymond Reiter (1980)

Linear Logic

Book: "Linear Logic" by Jean-Yves Girard (1987)

Substructural Logics

Book: "Substructural Logics" by Došen & Schroeder-Heister (1993)

Mathematical Logic

Model Theory

Book: "Model Theory" by Chen Chung Chang & Jerome Keisler (1973)

Proof Theory

Book: "Beweistheorie" by Gerhard Gentzen (1934-1935)

Recursion Theory (Computability Theory)

Book: "Theory of Recursive Functions and Effective Computability" by Hartley Rogers (1967)

Set Theory

Book: "Set Theory: An Introduction to Independence Proofs" by Kenneth Kunen (1980)

Category Theory

Book: "Categories for the Working Mathematician" by Saunders Mac Lane (1971)

Type Theory

Book: "Introduction to Type Theory" by Herman Geuvers (2011)

Constructive Mathematics

Book: "Constructive Analysis" by Errett Bishop & Douglas Bridges (1985)

Philosophical Logic

Philosophical Logic

Book: "Introduction to Philosophical Logic" by Anthony Grayling (1982)

Informal Logic

Book: "A Practical Study of Argument" by Trudy Govier (1985)

Dialectical Logic

Book: "Science of Logic" by G.W.F. Hegel (1812-1816)

Transcendental Logic (Kantian)

Book: "Critique of Pure Reason" by Immanuel Kant (1781)

Phenomenological Logic (Husserlian)

Book: "Formal and Transcendental Logic" by Edmund Husserl (1929)

Hermeneutic Logic

Book: "Truth and Method" by Hans-Georg Gadamer (1960)

Applied Logic

Computational Logic

Book: "Handbook of Practical Logic and Automated Reasoning" by John Harrison (2009)

Programming Logic

Book: "Logic in Computer Science" by Huth & Ryan (2004)

Database Logic

Book: "Foundation for Object/Relational Databases: The Third Manifesto" by Date & Darwen (1998)

Book: "Foundations of Logic Programming" by John Lloyd (1984)

Description Logic

Book: "The Description Logic Handbook" by Baader, Calvanese, McGuinness, Nardi & Patel-Schneider (2003)

Dynamic Logic

Epistemic Logic

Book: "Knowledge and Belief" by Jaakko Hintikka (1962)

Doxastic Logic

Temporal Logic

Book: "Temporal Logic of Programs" by Amir Pnueli (1977)

Deontic Logic

Book: "Deontic Logic in Computer Science" by Meyer & Wieringa (1993)

Game Logic

Book: "Game Logic" by Rohit Parikh (1985)

Belief Revision Theory
