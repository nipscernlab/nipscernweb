Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10.09.2024

At NIPSCERN Lab, we respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining transparency. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users.

1. Data Collection

NIPSCERN Lab does not collect any personal information from visitors or users. We do not use cookies, tracking mechanisms, or analytics tools that gather personal data.

2. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including our partners like CNPq, UFJF, and CERN. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before engaging with them.

3. Open Source Philosophy

All projects developed at NIPSCERN Lab are open-source. We do not track or monitor who accesses or downloads our software.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there are no user rights related to personal information in our systems.

5. Contact Information

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please reach out to us at contact@nipscern.com.
