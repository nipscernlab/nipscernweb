# THE ARCHITECT: A 6-Year Construction of the Complete Mind

**Objective:** A synthesis of Logic (Depth/Structure) and Culture (Breadth/Content).
**Method:** * **Logic:** Rigorous study, problem-solving, and mathematical derivation. Aiming for Homotopy Type Theory.
* **Culture:** "Curatorial Imersion." Wide reading of narratives combined with a daily sensory deluge of art, music, and poetry.
